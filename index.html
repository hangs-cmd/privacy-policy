Mood Mission Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how 'Mood Mission' (hereinafter "the App") handles user privacy. The App takes user privacy very seriously and is committed to protecting it safely.

1. Personal Information Collection and Methods

Information Collected: None.

This App does not collect, store, or require any personal information for registration, login, or any other purposes.

2. Camera Permissions and Face Data Processing Policy

To provide facial expression analysis features, the App uses your device's Camera permission and face data. The policy regarding the processing of face data is as follows:

  • On-device Processing: All facial recognition and expression analysis are performed entirely and exclusively in real-time within the user's device (utilizing MediaPipe), and are never transmitted to or stored on any external servers.
  • Purpose: The data is used solely for the real-time analysis of the user's facial expressions (e.g., happiness, sadness, surprise) to determine the success of the app's missions.
  • Data Destruction: Immediately upon frame analysis, the data is destroyed from memory and is not retained upon exiting the app or switching to the background.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not provide, sell, or share the generated expression data or original images with any third parties under any circumstances.

3. Third-Party Provision and Entrustment of Personal Information

As the App fundamentally does not collect any personal information from users, there is no information arbitrarily provided or entrusted to any third parties.

4. User Rights and How to Exercise Them

Users can revoke the granted camera permission at any time via their mobile device's settings ('Settings > Apps > Mood Mission > Permissions'). Additionally, users can immediately cease all service usage and permission access by deleting the App.

5. Information Destruction Procedures and Methods

Since no user data is stored on our servers, there is no personal information to destroy. Simply deleting the App from your Android device will safely erase all local data associated with the App.
